By UI-UX designer, M.F. Saathof

The software is designed with a number of rules always in mind, this for a real user feeling and experience.(UI-UX design)

  • Operating on regardless of which device: PC, tablet, mobile or regardless which operating system: IOS or Android.
  • 'Less is more', no visible information which you do not need or can't use.
  • Information on user 'need to know base'.
  • Simple operations,"my grandmother must be able to understand and operate it."
  • Operating with one hand.(mobile device)
  • Goal-oriented search, click as little as possible.
  • User must be able to read in their own language.
  • The possibility, in addition to the light/dark mode, to show something of our 'design' capabilities with the "awesome" mode.
